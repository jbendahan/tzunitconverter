Conversion Guides - How to Convert Units

How to Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit

Converting Celsius to Fahrenheit is one of the most common temperature conversions. Here's the simple formula and step-by-step process:

Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Step 1: Multiply the Celsius temperature by 9

Step 2: Divide the result by 5

Step 3: Add 32 to get the Fahrenheit temperature

Example: 25°C × 9/5 + 32 = 77°F

Quick Reference: 0°C = 32°F, 100°C = 212°F, body temperature 37°C = 98.6°F

How to Convert Fahrenheit to Celsius

Converting Fahrenheit to Celsius is the reverse process. This conversion is essential for cooking, weather, and scientific applications.

Formula: °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Step 1: Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature

Step 2: Multiply the result by 5

Step 3: Divide by 9 to get the Celsius temperature

Example: 77°F - 32 × 5/9 = 25°C

Memory Tip: Room temperature is about 20°C (68°F), freezing is 0°C (32°F)

How to Convert Feet to Meters

Converting feet to meters is crucial for international measurements, construction, and travel planning.

Formula: meters = feet × 0.3048

Quick Method: Divide feet by 3.28 to get approximate meters

Examples:

• 6 feet = 1.83 meters (average person height)

• 10 feet = 3.05 meters (standard ceiling height)

• 100 feet = 30.48 meters (building height)

Reverse Conversion: meters × 3.28 = feet

How to Convert Meters to Feet

This conversion is essential when dealing with US construction, real estate, and sports measurements.

Formula: feet = meters × 3.28084

Quick Method: Multiply meters by 3.28 for approximate feet

Examples:

• 1.8 meters = 5.9 feet (average person height)

• 3 meters = 9.84 feet (room length)

• 100 meters = 328 feet (track length)

Sports Reference: Olympic pool is 50m = 164 feet long

How to Convert Pounds to Kilograms

Converting pounds to kilograms is essential for international weight measurements, shipping, and health tracking.

Formula: kg = pounds ÷ 2.205

Quick Method: Divide pounds by 2.2 for approximate kg

Examples:

• 150 lbs = 68 kg (average adult weight)

• 50 lbs = 23 kg (luggage weight limit)

• 200 lbs = 91 kg (fitness goal weight)

Medical Use: Most medical equipment worldwide uses kilograms

How to Convert Kilograms to Pounds

This conversion is needed when traveling to the US or using American fitness equipment and recipes.

Formula: pounds = kg × 2.205

Quick Method: Multiply kg by 2.2 for approximate pounds

Examples:

• 70 kg = 154 lbs (average adult weight)

• 25 kg = 55 lbs (standard luggage weight)

• 1 kg = 2.2 lbs (quick reference)

Fitness Tip: Gym weights are often marked in both units

How to Convert Miles to Kilometers

Essential for travel, running, and understanding international distances.

Formula: km = miles × 1.609

Quick Method: Multiply miles by 1.6 for approximate km

Examples:

• 1 mile = 1.61 km (basic conversion)

• 10 miles = 16.1 km (10K race distance)

• 26.2 miles = 42.2 km (marathon distance)

Travel Tip: Highway speed limits: 60 mph ≈ 97 km/h

How to Convert Kilometers to Miles

Useful when traveling to the US or understanding American distance measurements.

Formula: miles = km ÷ 1.609

Quick Method: Multiply km by 0.62 for approximate miles

Examples:

• 5 km = 3.1 miles (5K race distance)

• 100 km = 62 miles (highway distance)

• 42.2 km = 26.2 miles (marathon distance)

Running Reference: 10K = 6.2 miles, half-marathon = 13.1 miles

How Many Ounces in a Pound Calculator

Quick Answer: 1 pound = 16 ounces

This is one of the most fundamental weight conversions in the Imperial system.

Why 16 Ounces = 1 Pound:

This relationship dates back to the Roman libra (pound), which was divided into 12 unciae (ounces). The modern system uses 16 ounces per pound, established in medieval England.

Common Pound to Ounce Conversions:

Practical Applications:

• Postal and shipping weights

• Baby weight tracking

Convert 180 cm to Feet and Inches

Quick Answer: 180 cm = 5 feet 11 inches

180 cm is a commonly referenced height because it's considered tall in most countries (about 5'11").

Step-by-step conversion:

1. Convert cm to inches: 180 ÷ 2.54 = 70.87 inches

2. Divide by 12 for feet: 70.87 ÷ 12 = 5.91 feet

3. The remainder is inches: 0.91 × 12 = 11 inches

4. Result: 5 feet 11 inches

Height Comparisons:

175 cm = 5'9" (average male height in US)

180 cm = 5'11" (tall height)

185 cm = 6'1" (very tall height)
